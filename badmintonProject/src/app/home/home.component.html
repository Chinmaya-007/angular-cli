Badminton Association of India(BAI)

About Badminton Association of India.

Badminton Association of India (BAI) is the governing body Badminton in India. Based in Lucknow, BAI is an association registered under the societies act. It was formed in 1934 and has been holding national-level tournaments in India since 1936. BAI has 28 state members that conduct Badminton tournaments and have a 2x voting power compared to the 8 affiliate members, who don't conduct tournaments and have a single vote each in the association.
